About Our Correspondent

Haji Mr. N. Abdul Azeez is a dedicated and visionary leader who has been at the forefront of our institution's growth and development. With a steadfast commitment to educational excellence, he has played a pivotal role in shaping the futures of countless students. His passion for education and community service is evident in every initiative he undertakes.

Mr. Azeez's leadership is characterized by his unwavering dedication to creating a nurturing and inclusive learning environment. He believes in the power of education to transform lives and is committed to providing opportunities for students from all backgrounds to succeed. Under his guidance, our institution has achieved remarkable milestones and continues to strive for greater heights.

In addition to his contributions to education, Mr. Azeez is also known for his philanthropic efforts. He actively participates in various community service projects and works tirelessly to uplift the underprivileged. His vision and leadership inspire everyone around him to aim higher and achieve more.

Jeevan's School

Jeevan's School is dedicated to fostering an inclusive and stimulating learning environment. Our mission is to provide top-notch education that blends academic rigor with holistic development.
