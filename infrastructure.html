Facilities in Our School

Welcome to Our School's Science Lab!


Our school is proud to offer a state-of-the-art science laboratory where students can engage in hands-on learning, conduct experiments, and explore the wonders of science in a safe and inspiring environment.

  • Cutting-Edge Equipment:Equipped with modern scientific instruments and apparatus, our lab provides students with access to the latest tools for conducting experiments across various scientific disciplines.

  • Safe and Controlled Environment: Safety is our top priority. Our lab adheres to strict safety protocols and is equipped with safety goggles, lab coats, emergency showers, and eye wash stations to ensure a secure learning environment.

  • Diverse Experimentation:From biology and chemistry to physics and environmental science, our lab supports a wide range of scientific exploration. Students have the opportunity to engage in diverse experiments and projects that foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

  • Interactive Learning:Our lab promotes interactive learning experiences where students can collaborate with their peers, ask questions, and engage in discussions that deepen their understanding of scientific principles.

Welcome to Our School's Computer Lab!


Our school's computer lab is a hub of digital learning and innovation where students can explore, create, and collaborate in a dynamic technological environment. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and resources, our computer lab offers a wide range of opportunities for students to enhance their digital literacy skills and prepare for success in the 21st century.

  • High-Speed Internet Access: Our computer lab is equipped with high-speed internet connectivity, allowing students to access online resources, research materials, and educational websites quickly and efficiently.

  • Modern Workstations: Each workstation in our computer lab is equipped with the latest hardware and software, including desktop computers, monitors, keyboards, and mice, providing students with a seamless and productive computing experience.

  • Software Applications: Students have access to a comprehensive suite of software applications for academic and creative pursuits, including productivity tools, multimedia editing software, programming environments, and more.

  • Multimedia Resources:Our computer lab is equipped with multimedia resources such as digital cameras, microphones, scanners, and printers, enabling students to engage in multimedia projects, presentations, and digital storytelling.

Welcome to the English Lab!


At our school,we believe in fostering a deep appreciation for language and literature. Our English Lab is dedicated to providing students with the tools and resources they need to become proficient communicators, critical thinkers, and lifelong learners.

  • Book Clubs: Students have the opportunity to join book clubs where they explore a variety of literary genres, discuss themes, and share insights.

  • Author Studies: Our curriculum includes in-depth studies of renowned authors, allowing students to explore their works and literary contributions.

  • Grammar and Vocabulary: We offer targeted lessons and activities to enhance grammar skills, expand vocabulary, and improve language fluency.

  • Speaking and Presentation Skills:Through debates, presentations, and public speaking exercises, students build confidence and effective communication skills.

Welcome to Library!


Our library serves as a hub of knowledge, exploration, and learning for students, faculty, and staff. With a vast collection of resources and a variety of services, our library supports academic excellence and fosters a love for reading and research.

  • Books: Explore our extensive collection of fiction and non-fiction books covering a wide range of subjects and genres. From classic literature to the latest bestsellers, there's something for everyone.

  • E-Books and Digital Resourcess: Access our digital library from anywhere, anytime. Browse e-books, audiobooks, online databases, and scholarly journals to support research and learning.

  • Reference Materials:Find encyclopedias, dictionaries, atlases, and other reference materials to aid in research and classroom assignments.

  • Periodicals and Magazines:Periodicals and Magazines: Stay informed and up-to-date with our collection of newspapers, magazines, and academic journals.

Welcome to Our Medical Facilities!


At our school,health and well-being of our students are of utmost importance. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive medical facilities and services to ensure the physical and emotional well-being of all students. Our on-campus medical facilities are staffed by experienced healthcare professionals who are committed to providing quality care and support to our students

  • School Nurse:Our qualified school nurse is available on campus during school hours to provide first aid, assess minor illnesses and injuries, and offer health education and counseling.

  • Health Check-ups: We conduct regular health check-ups to monitor students' overall health and identify any potential health concerns early on.

  • Immunization Programs: We adhere to national immunization guidelines and offer immunization programs to ensure students are protected against preventable diseases.

  • Emergency Response:Our medical team is trained to handle medical emergencies efficiently and effectively. We have protocols in place to respond to emergencies and provide immediate medical attention when needed.

Welcome to Our School Auditorium!


Our school auditorium is a central hub for creativity, expression, and community at Jeevan's School. With state-of-the-art facilities and a vibrant atmosphere, it serves as the perfect venue for a wide range of events, performances, and gatherings.

  • Spacious Seating:Our auditorium boasts comfortable seating arrangements, providing ample space for large audiences to enjoy performances and presentations.

  • Stage and Lighting: Equipped with a professional stage and advanced lighting systems, our auditorium offers the perfect setting for theatrical productions, concerts, and speeches.

  • School Assemblies: Our auditorium hosts regular school assemblies, bringing together students, teachers, and staff for important announcements, celebrations, and guest speakers.

  • Performing Arts:From drama productions and musical concerts to dance recitals and talent shows, our auditorium showcases the talents of our students and faculty.

Welcome to Our School's Transport Facilities!


We prioritize the safety and convenience of our students, and our transport services are designed to provide reliable transportation to and from school. Our well-maintained fleet of buses is equipped with modern amenities and staffed by experienced drivers and attendants who ensure a comfortable journey for our students.

  • Safe and Reliable Transportation: Safety is our top priority. Our buses undergo regular maintenance checks and adhere to all safety standards to ensure a secure journey for our students.

  • Dedicated Routes: We operate a network of dedicated bus routes covering various neighborhoods to accommodate students from different areas.

  • Experienced Staff: Our drivers and attendants are trained professionals who prioritize the well-being of our students. They are courteous, responsible, and always ready to assist students during their commute.

  • Modern Amenities:Our buses are equipped with modern amenities such as comfortable seating, air conditioning/heating, and GPS tracking systems for real-time monitoring.
